FAQ
How long is my key valid?
Each key is valid for 24 hours from the time it is generated. After expiry, simply go through the Linkvertise process again to get a new key.
Why do I need to go through Linkvertise?
Linkvertise is how NXT HUB supports its infrastructure and keeps all scripts free. It also helps prevent automated abuse of our systems.
My key is not working — what do I do?
Make sure you copied the key correctly without extra spaces. If it still doesn't work, try generating a new key. Keys expire after 24 hours.
Can I share my key with others?
Keys are personal and tied to your session. Sharing them may cause issues. Each user should generate their own key.
